About Me

Hello, I'm Romina Soledad Molina

Hi, I'm Romina Soledad Molina — scientist, mindful technologist, and bridge builder between AI, embedded systems and human-centered innovation.

As a postdoctoral researcher at MLab-STI/ICTP, I work at the intersection of machine learning, reconfigurable computing and embedded hardware. My focus is on developing energy-efficient Edge AI solutions — particularly FPGA-based systems — for real-time scientific applications.

I care deeply about creating technology that is both powerful and accessible: from optimizing deep learning models on constrained platforms, to designing intuitive interfaces that bring intelligence closer to humans.

Beyond the technical, I’m passionate about interdisciplinary collaboration, ethical AI, and building tools that contribute to collective wellbeing. I believe that innovation is most impactful when it integrates purpose, precision, and compassion.
